## **🔹 Funtend Tokenomics Plan ($FNTD) 🚀**  

A well-structured tokenomics model ensures **sustainability, user incentives, and long-term value creation**. Below is the best tokenomics plan for **$FNTD**, considering both on-chain and off-chain revenue models, ecosystem growth, and incentives for creators, brands, and users.  

---

## **1️⃣ Token Overview**  

- **Token Name:** Funtend  
- **Ticker:** $FNTD  
- **Blockchain:** Polygon (Ethereum L2)  
- **Total Supply:** **1,000,000,000 $FNTD (fixed, no minting after TGE)**  
- **Token Type:** Utility & Governance  
- **Contract Standard:** ERC-20  

---

## **2️⃣ Utility of $FNTD**  

🔹 **Payments:**  
- Purchase digital & physical streetwear on the Funtend marketplace.  
- Pay for AI-generated design tools & customization.  

🔹 **Governance:**  
- $FNTD holders **vote on new collections, collaborations, and roadmap decisions**.  
- Community-driven fashion trends via DAO voting.  

🔹 **Staking & Rewards:**  
- Users stake $FNTD to **access premium features** (AI enhancements, exclusive designs).  
- Designers stake $FNTD to **boost their collections’ visibility**.  

🔹 **Royalty & Creator Economy:**  
- **Smart contract royalties** auto-distribute $FNTD to designers on resale.  
- Influencers earn $FNTD **through affiliate marketing & content engagement**.  

🔹 **Sustainability Incentives:**  
- Users **return old apparel for on-chain recycling rewards**.  
- Earn $FNTD for eco-friendly actions (e.g., choosing sustainable materials).  

🔹 **Metaverse & Interoperability:**  
- NFT-linked fashion assets work across **Decentraland, Sandbox, Lens Protocol & AR filters**.

## **4️⃣ Revenue Model (On-Chain & Off-Chain)**  

### **🔹 On-Chain Revenue Streams**
- **Marketplace Fees (2-5%)** on every NFT streetwear transaction.  
- **AI Tool Subscriptions:** Pay-per-use or premium plans in $FNTD.  
- **Staking & Memberships:** Users and brands stake $FNTD for premium access.  
- **Brand Collaborations:** Web3-native brands pay to launch limited-edition collabs.  

### **🔹 Off-Chain Revenue Streams**
- **Physical Fashion Sales:** Hybrid Web3-native apparel purchases.  
- **NFC-Enabled Authentication:** Brands integrate Funtend’s NFC-chip for product verification.  
- **Retail Partnerships:** Funtend-powered Web3 stores in real-world retail locations.

## **5️⃣ Token Burning & Deflationary Mechanics**  
- **10% of all marketplace fees go to burning** $FNTD to control inflation.  
- Recycling programs **lock tokens in a sustainability fund** to reward eco-friendly behavior.  
- **Dynamic buyback mechanism** using a portion of revenue to stabilize the token economy.
