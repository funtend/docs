[Funtend](https://funtend.xyz) is transforming the streetwear industry by integrating AI-generated fashion design, NFT-based digital ownership, and decentralized social commerce. Built on **Polygon PoS**, our protocol enables users to design unique streetwear using machine-learning-driven generative AI, mint these designs as NFTs with on-chain metadata, and redeem them for NFC-embedded apparel that links back to the blockchain for provenance and authenticity.

The $185B streetwear industry suffers from mass production waste, counterfeiting, and limited personalization. Funtend addresses these inefficiencies with on-chain verifiable fashion assets, fractional ownership models for co-created designs, and a DAO-governed royalty structure for designers.

Our AI stack leverages GANs and diffusion models fine-tuned for textile pattern generation, while our smart contracts utilize **ERC-6551** (Token Bound Accounts) and **PBT** (Physical Backed Tokens) to enable digital-physical interoperability. We integrate Lens Protocol V2 for decentralized social engagement, incentivizing creators via on-chain reputation scoring and tokenized rewards.
